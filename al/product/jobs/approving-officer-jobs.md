# Jobs to Be Done: Approving Officer

Format: "When [situation], I want to [motivation], so I can [expected outcome]."

---

**Job 1 — Queue triage**
When new pass requests arrive throughout the day alongside my other duties, I want to see all pending requests in a single prioritised view sorted by requested visit time, so I can process them in the correct order without missing time-sensitive requests.

---

**Job 2 — Identity and purpose evaluation**
When I open a pass request to review it, I want to see the visitor's stated purpose, their government ID photo, and their ID number (last 4 digits) in one view without navigating between screens, so I can make an informed approval or rejection decision in under 60 seconds.

---

**Job 3 — Visitor notification on decision**
When I approve or reject a request, I want the visitor to be notified automatically and immediately with the outcome and any relevant details (approved time window or rejection reason), so the visitor can act on the decision without calling me or waiting for a manual message.

---

**Job 4 — Absence delegation**
When I am going on leave or will be unavailable during working hours, I want to assign a named backup officer to handle my pending queue before I leave, so requests do not stall and visitors are not kept waiting indefinitely.

---

**Job 5 — Audit response**
When a compliance officer or supervisor asks me to account for a specific approval I made, I want to retrieve a timestamped record of what information I saw and what decision I took, so I can demonstrate due diligence without relying on memory or manual logs.
