# Jobs to Be Done: Gate Security

Format: "When [situation], I want to [motivation], so I can [expected outcome]."

---

**Job 1 — Fast QR verification**
When a visitor presents their pass at the gate and there is a queue behind them, I want to scan the QR code and see a clear green or red result within 2 seconds, so I can make the entry decision and keep the queue moving without delay.

---

**Job 2 — Scan failure handling**
When a visitor's QR code cannot be read by the scanner — due to a cracked screen, poor brightness, or a printed copy — I want a clear on-screen fallback option that lets me look up the pass by name or ID number, so I can still verify them correctly without turning them away or calling a supervisor.

---

**Job 3 — Unambiguous denial**
When a visitor presents an expired, already-used, or out-of-window pass, I want the terminal to display the specific reason in large clear text with no entry action available, so I can communicate the denial to the visitor with confidence and without ambiguity.

---

**Job 4 — Automatic entry logging**
When I grant entry to a visitor, I want the system to automatically record the exact timestamp and my guard ID without any extra step from me, so the audit log is always complete and I am never responsible for maintaining a parallel manual register.

---

**Job 5 — Shift handover**
When my shift ends and the next guard is taking over, I want to see a summary of all entries, exits, exceptions, and manual fallback events I processed during my shift, so I can hand over accurately and the incoming guard knows exactly what happened.
