# Jobs to Be Done: Visitor

Format: "When [situation], I want to [motivation], so I can [expected outcome]."

---

**Job 1 — Pre-arrival request**
When I need to visit a government facility for a scheduled meeting or appointment, I want to submit my pass request from my phone before I leave home, so I can arrive at the gate with an approved QR code and avoid standing in a queue.

---

**Job 2 — Gate entry**
When I arrive at the gate and the security guard is ready to verify me, I want to show a single QR code on my screen without needing to explain my visit or show paper documents, so I can enter quickly and without friction.

---

**Job 3 — Status tracking**
When my pass request is pending and I am unsure whether to start travelling, I want to see the current approval status on my phone in real time, so I can decide whether to leave now or wait without needing to call anyone.

---

**Job 4 — Rejection recovery**
When my pass request is rejected and I have already planned my visit, I want to see the specific reason for rejection and clear next steps in plain language, so I can either correct my application and re-submit or contact the right person without confusion.

---

**Job 5 — Repeat visit efficiency**
When I visit the same facility regularly and already have a verified profile in the system, I want my personal details and ID to be pre-filled on subsequent requests, so I can request a new pass in under a minute without re-entering everything from scratch.
